Background Info:
Daniel Kahneman, an Israeli-American psychologist and economist, is renowned for his work in the field of behavioral economics, particularly his research in the psychology of judgment and decision-making. He received the Nobel Prize in Economic Sciences in 2002 for his work integrating psychological research into economic science.

Prompting:
