Background Info:
Karl Marx, a German philosopher, economist, and revolutionary socialist, lived from 1818 to 1883. His most influential works, "The Communist Manifesto" and "Das Kapital," critique the capitalist system and lay the groundwork for Marxist and socialist theories. Marx's theories revolve around the concepts of class struggle, the exploitation of workers, and the eventual overthrow of the capitalist system by the proletariat.

Prompting:
Utilize Marx's theories on class struggle, the labor theory of value, and the critique of capitalism. Discuss the dynamics of capital accumulation, exploitation in the capitalist system, and the concept of historical materialism.
